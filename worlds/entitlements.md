# Entitlements

**Entitlements** are things you buy in a BrickVerse world, like VIP, items, or in-game cash.

***

### 🧩 Types of Entitlements

#### 🌟 World Passes (Gamepasses)

* One-time purchase
* You keep it **forever**
* Example: VIP, special abilities, vehicles, DLC

***

#### 🔁 World Consumables (Products)

* Can be bought **multiple times**
* Used or consumed
* Example: in-game cash, boosts

***

#### ⏳ World Memberships (Subscriptions)

* Last **30 days**
* Need to renew to keep access
* Example: monthly VIP

***

### ⚠️ Notes

* Entitlements are **per world**
* Memberships automatically expire
* Passes are permanent\*

{% hint style="warning" %}
World Developers and BrickVerse Staff can revoke access to entitlements.
{% endhint %}
