# Creator Analytics Terms of Use

Creator Analytics is a core feature of BrickVerse and is **automatically enabled for all experiences** on the platform. By creating or operating an experience on BrickVerse, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

***

#### 1. Purpose of Data Collection

BrickVerse collects and processes data related to user interactions with experiences for the purpose of providing analytics and improving the platform.

This includes, but is not limited to:

* Discovery and attraction (how users find your experience)
* Engagement (session time, interactions, retention)
* Monetization (in-experience purchases, conversions)
* Page views and traffic patterns
* Feature usage and performance metrics

All data is used strictly for **internal analytics, platform development, and platform-related marketing purposes**.

***

#### 2. Child-Focused Data Protections

BrickVerse is a platform primarily used by children and younger audiences. As such:

* Data is handled in accordance with applicable child privacy and data protection laws
* Creators **do not receive personally identifiable information (PII)** about users
* All analytics provided to creators are **aggregated and anonymized**
* Data is never used to directly identify or target individual users

***

#### 3. No Sale or Commercial Use of Data

BrickVerse does **not sell, rent, or trade user data**.

Creators are strictly prohibited from:

* Selling, licensing, or monetizing analytics data
* Using analytics data for external advertising or targeting outside of BrickVerse
* Combining analytics data with external data sources to identify users

Analytics data exists solely to help improve experiences within BrickVerse.

***

#### 4. Creator Responsibilities

By using BrickVerse, you agree that you will:

* Use analytics data only to improve your experience(s) on the platform
* Not attempt to identify, track, or profile individual users
* Not export or distribute analytics data outside of BrickVerse
* Not misuse data in any way that violates user privacy or platform policies

Violation of these requirements may result in enforcement actions.

***

#### 5. Benchmarking & Confidentiality

Analytics dashboards may include comparative or benchmarking insights.

You agree that:

* Benchmarking data is **confidential and proprietary to BrickVerse**
* It may only be used internally to evaluate your experience’s performance
* It may not be shared, published, or disclosed in any form

***

#### 6. Data Minimization & Security

BrickVerse limits data collection to what is necessary and ensures that data is:

* Processed securely and responsibly
* Retained only as long as needed
* Used exclusively for analytics, safety, and platform improvement

***

#### 7. Enforcement
