# Data Retention and Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2026\
**Last Updated:** April 2026

***

### 1. Introduction

This Data Retention and Deletion Policy (“Policy”) describes the practices of **BrickVerse**, operated by Meta Games LLC (“BrickVerse,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), regarding the retention, anonymization, and deletion of data collected through the BrickVerse platform.

This Policy is intended to ensure that data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill operational, security, legal, and compliance obligations, and is deleted in a timely and consistent manner thereafter.

***

### 2. Automated Deletion Process

BrickVerse operates an automated data lifecycle management system that performs scheduled deletion and cleanup tasks.

* Deletion processes are executed **once daily at approximately 00:00 (midnight) server time**.
* Data exceeding defined retention thresholds is **permanently deleted or irreversibly anonymized**, as applicable.
* These processes are enforced consistently across all applicable systems and services.

***

### 3. Data Retention Periods

BrickVerse retains different categories of data for varying periods based on their purpose, sensitivity, and operational necessity.

#### 3.1 Thirty (30) Day Retention

The following data is retained for a maximum of **30 days**:

* World chat logs

Such data is permanently deleted after the expiration of the retention period.

***

#### 3.2 Seventy-Eight (78) Hour Retention

The following data is retained for a maximum of approximately **78 hours (3.25 days)**:

* Link preview cache data

This data is automatically purged after expiration due to its temporary and performance-related nature.

***

#### 3.3 Ninety (90) Day Retention

The following data is retained for a maximum of **90 days**:

* Notifications
* Pending friend requests
* User reports
* Guild join requests
* Platform audit logs
* Bug reports and bounty submissions
* Support tickets (limited to those marked as “Closed” or “Resolved”)
* Support ticket messages

Such data is permanently deleted after the applicable retention period unless required for ongoing investigations, legal obligations, or enforcement actions.

***

#### 3.4 One Hundred Eighty (180) Day Retention

The following data is retained for a maximum of **180 days**:

* Forum posts
* Asset comments
* Guild audit logs
* Login history
* Hardware identifier (HWID) logs
* IP address logs
* Social chat messages

For social chat messages, BrickVerse may implement a **soft deletion mechanism**, whereby messages are marked as deleted and no longer accessible to users after the retention period.

***

### 4. Data Deletion and Anonymization

Upon expiration of the applicable retention period:

* Data is **permanently deleted** from active systems; or
* Where deletion is not immediately feasible, data may be **irreversibly anonymized or de-identified** such that it can no longer be associated with an individual user.

Deleted data is not recoverable through standard platform functionality.

***

### 5. Exceptions

Notwithstanding the foregoing, BrickVerse may retain data beyond the stated retention periods where necessary to:

* Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
* Enforce platform Terms of Service or other policies
* Detect, prevent, or investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents
* Resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Such retained data will be limited to the minimum necessary scope and duration required for the applicable purpose.

***

### 6. Security and Integrity

BrickVerse implements reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to ensure that:

* Data deletion processes are executed securely and reliably
* Residual data is not accessible after deletion
* Retention policies are consistently enforced across systems

***

### 7. Changes to This Policy

BrickVerse reserves the right to modify or update this Policy at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting or implementation unless otherwise required by applicable law.
