# Sponsored Content on Brickverse

**Sponsored Content** allows creators, brands, or organizations to **pay for promotional placement** within Brickverse. This ensures that your content reaches a wider audience, but it is **different from Featured Content**, which is curated by staff.

***

### üíµ How Sponsored Content Works

* **Paid promotion:** Anyone can pay to have their guild, market item, or game highlighted in prominent areas.
* **Placement:** Sponsored content may appear on the homepage, in discovery sections, or alongside other content.
* **Visibility:** Paid placement does **not guarantee curation or quality approval** beyond basic safety checks.

***

| Aspect                | Featured Content                                               | Sponsored Content                                   |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Selection**         | Curated by staff for quality, creativity, and community impact | Paid placement by advertiser                        |
| **Visibility reason** | Recognized for excellence or popularity                        | Paid to increase reach                              |
| **Eligibility**       | Must meet staff standards                                      | Open to anyone who pays\* (subject to safety rules) |
| **Recognition**       | Staff endorsement                                              | Advertising label displayed                         |

{% hint style="success" %}
Sponsored content is clearly labeled so users can distinguish it from staff-selected features.
{% endhint %}

***

### ‚úÖ Guidelines for Sponsored Content

* All sponsored uploads must still follow **Brickverse rules** and be safe for the community.
* Sponsored content cannot contain offensive or inappropriate material.
* Misleading or scammy promotions are **not allowed** and may result in removal or account penalties.

***

### üìå Summary

Sponsored content is a way to **promote your content to more users** quickly, but it is **not the same as being featured by staff**. Featured content reflects **quality and creativity**, while sponsored content reflects **paid promotion**.
