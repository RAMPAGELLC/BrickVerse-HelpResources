## Introduction

BrickVerse's ID Verified Badge helpers players on our platform easily identify a player who has verified their identity through the BrickVerse Government Identification Verification System.

Players possessing this badge are not noteable figures, brickverse staff, or government officials.

## How can I get it?

Unfortunately, this badge is handed out at the discretion of BrickVerse automatically. We do not do applications for this badge.
