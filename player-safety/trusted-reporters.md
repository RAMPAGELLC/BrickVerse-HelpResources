# Trusted Reporters

The **Trusted Reporter** system rewards users who consistently help keep BrickVerse safe by submitting accurate moderation reports. Trusted Reporters receive faster report handling and stronger moderation impact when reporting harmful content.

#### How to Become a Trusted Reporter

You may become eligible for Trusted Reporter status in either of these ways:

* Submit a consistent history of **valid reports** that result in moderation action.
* **Verify your identity** and submit **at least one report every 30 days**.

Eligibility is reviewed automatically **every day at 12:00 AM server time**.

#### Activity Requirements

To keep Trusted Reporter status active, you must continue submitting reports regularly:

* At least **one report every 30 days** is required to remain active.
* Inactive accounts may automatically lose Trusted Reporter privileges.
* Eligibility is continuously re-evaluated during daily checks.
* Additional eligibility requirements such as Moderation History against your account and account age.

#### Benefits of Trusted Reporter Status

Trusted Reporters receive several moderation advantages:

* Reports are **prioritized faster** by both human moderators and automated moderation systems.
* Reports from Trusted Reporters are given additional confidence during review.
* If enough Trusted Reporters report the same content, it may be **automatically moderated before human review**.

#### Automatic Moderation

When multiple Trusted Reporters report the same content:

* The content may be temporarily moderated or restricted automatically.
* A human moderator will still review the action afterward.
* Automatic actions help reduce response time for serious violations.

#### Important Notes

Trusted Reporter status does **not** guarantee every report will result in action.

**Submitting false, abusive, or misleading reports will result in account termination.**

Accurate reporting helps protect the community for everyone.
