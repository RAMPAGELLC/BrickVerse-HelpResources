# Understanding Frozen Transactions and Rollback Transactions in Brickverse

At Brickverse, some account transactions may occasionally be adjusted or restricted by staff to protect users, maintain marketplace integrity, and comply with platform policies.

This article explains the difference between **Frozen Transactions (Holds)** and **Rollback Transactions**.

***

### Frozen Transactions (Also Called Holds)

A **Frozen Transaction** is a temporary staff action that places a transaction on hold before it is finalized.

#### Why a transaction may be frozen

Transactions may be frozen when they are:

* flagged as suspicious or unusual
* pending moderation review
* under fraud investigation
* awaiting legal or compliance review
* connected to disputed account activity
* linked to marketplace abuse or policy concerns

#### What happens during a freeze

When a transaction is frozen:

* the transfer is temporarily paused
* items, currency, or account value may not be accessible yet
* the transaction remains under staff review
* no final outcome is applied until the review is complete

#### Important to know

A frozen transaction **does not automatically mean a rule violation occurred**. It only means staff need additional time to verify the transaction before allowing it to proceed.

#### Possible outcomes after review

After review, staff may:

* release the transaction normally
* convert it into a rollback
* remove or cancel the transaction if necessary

***

### Rollback Transactions

A **Rollback Transaction** is a staff action that reverses a transaction after it has already occurred.

#### Why rollbacks happen

Rollbacks are usually issued for:

* refunds
* payment disputes
* accidental purchases
* unauthorized transactions
* fraud correction
* marketplace corrections

#### What happens during a rollback

When a rollback occurs:

* previously transferred currency, items, or value are returned
* the original transaction is reversed
* account balances may change immediately

#### Important to know

A rollback is a **completed corrective action**, unlike a frozen transaction which is temporary and pending/under review.

***

### Frozen vs Rollback: Quick Comparison

| Transaction Type     | Purpose                               | Temporary or Final |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Frozen Transaction   | Holds suspicious or reviewed activity | Temporary          |
| Rollback Transaction | Reverses completed activity           | Final              |

***

### Need Help?

If you believe a transaction on your account was frozen or rolled back incorrectly, contact Brickverse Support and include:

* your username
* transaction date
* item or currency involved
* any related payment details

This helps staff review your case faster.
