# UGC Creator Nameplate

### About

The **UGC Creator Nameplate** is a label displayed above the name of the creator or developer of a UGC item. This includes content such as Worlds/Experiences, Marketplace Items, and Developer Assets.

This feature helps clearly identify who created the item, making it easier for users to recognize and trust the original creator. It also improves transparency across the platform by ensuring proper attribution is always visible.

***

### How do I get it?

The UGC Creator Nameplate is applied **automatically**.

If you are the original creator of a UGC item, the nameplate will appear on your content without any additional setup or application required.

***

### Why it matters

* **Creator Recognition** – Ensures creators are properly credited for their work
* **User Trust** – Helps users identify official or authentic content
* **Transparency** – Clearly distinguishes creators from other users

***

### Preview

The nameplate appears directly above or alongside the creator’s name on supported items and experiences, making it immediately visible when viewing or interacting with the content.
