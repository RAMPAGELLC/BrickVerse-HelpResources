# BrickVerse Creator Fund (Beta)

The **BrickVerse Creator Fund** is a temporary program designed to reward developers for building engaging, high-quality experiences on the platform. The fund is **financed directly from platform profits** and distributes payouts based on real player engagement—not artificial metrics—while we complete regulatory compliance for our full Developer Exchange system.

***

### **How the Creator Fund is Funded**

The Creator Fund is funded monthly from a **dedicated portion of BrickVerse platform profits**. This includes revenue generated from:

* **Astro Membership subscriptions**
* **In-platform currency (Cubes) purchases**

Each month, BrickVerse allocates a **fixed budget from these profits** into the Creator Fund pool. This means:

* The total payout amount **scales with platform growth**
* Higher platform revenue = **larger Creator Fund pool**
* Lower revenue periods may result in **smaller available payouts**

This model ensures the fund remains **sustainable and directly tied to the success of the platform**, aligning developer earnings with overall ecosystem growth.

### **Developer Eligibility**

To participate, developers must:

* Be 18 years of age or older
* Complete Stripe KYC verification through Stripe Connect
* Be ID Verified on BrickVerse ([brickverse.gg/giv](https://brickverse.gg/giv))
* Have an active Astro Membership
* Remain in good standing and comply with our Terms of Service

### **Experience Requirements (Game-Level)**

To qualify for payouts, experiences must meet the following:

* Be **public and discoverable** (eligible for organic traffic)
* Maintain a minimum **average CCU of 30+ players** (measured over a 7–30 day rolling period)
  * 30 CCU = baseline eligibility
  * Higher CCU = increased share of payouts
* Achieve at least **100 weekly unique players**
* Generate **500+ monthly qualified playtime hours**
* Maintain **D1 retention of 15% or higher**
* Maintain **average session length of 8+ minutes**
* Avoid any mechanics that encourage **idle farming or artificial engagement**
* Pass **automated and/or manual fraud review**

### **Qualified Playtime (Counted Engagement Only)**

Only legitimate engagement is counted toward payouts. Playtime must meet all of the following:

* Player account age ≥ **14 days**
* Session length between **2–120 minutes**
* Player demonstrates **active gameplay/input**
* Maximum **2 hours of counted playtime per user per day**
* Player did not join from cross-experience teleportation
  * This is specifically about the BrickLua API to teleport player(s) between experiences.

The following are excluded:

* AFK or idle time
* Bot or alt-account activity (shared IP/device patterns)
* Abnormal behavior (instant rejoins, 24/7 sessions, scripted loops)

### **How Payouts Are Determined**

The Creator Fund operates as a **monthly pool**, and payouts are distributed proportionally:

* Each experience earns a **score** based on:
  * Qualified Playtime (primary factor)
  * Average CCU (consistency of players)
  * Engagement quality (retention, session length, return visits)
*   Your payout is calculated as:

    > **(Your Experience Score ÷ Total Platform Score) × Monthly Fund Pool**

This means:

* There is **no fixed rate per hour or player**
* Games with **real, sustained engagement** earn a larger share
* Low-quality or suspicious traffic is **discounted or removed**

### **Payout Process (Manual Requests)**

* Payouts are calculated **once per calendar month**
* Developers must **manually request payout** through the dashboard
* Each developer may request payout **once per month**
* All requests undergo **review for fraud and metric validation**
* Approved payouts are processed via **Stripe**
