# About the Developer Toolbox

The **Developer Toolbox** — also known as the **Toolbox Marketplace** — is a centralized library of assets that creators can use to build experiences faster and more efficiently on the platform.

Instead of starting everything from scratch, developers can browse, purchase, or download ready-made content created by other users and official sources.

***

### 📦 What You Can Find

The Toolbox includes a wide variety of assets, such as:

* **🧩 Scripts** – Pre-built functionality like movement systems, UI logic, or game mechanics
* **🧱 Models & Meshes** – 3D objects, props, characters, and environment pieces
* **🎨 Textures & Materials** – Surface details, decals, and visual effects
* **🔊 Sounds & Audio** – Music tracks, sound effects, ambient audio
* **🎮 Complete Systems** – Full kits like inventory systems, weapons, or UI frameworks

These assets can be either:

* **Free** – Available for anyone to use
* **Paid** – Created by developers who sell their work on the marketplace

***

### 🚀 Why Use the Toolbox?

Using the Developer Toolbox can significantly speed up development:

* **Save Time** – Skip building common systems from scratch
* **Learn Faster** – Study how other developers structure their assets
* **Prototype Quickly** – Test ideas using pre-made content
* **Enhance Quality** – Use high-quality assets to improve your experience

***

### 🧑‍💻 For Creators & Sellers

The Toolbox isn’t just for downloading — it’s also a place to **publish your own work**.

Developers can:

* Upload assets they’ve created
* Set pricing (free or paid)
* Earn from their creations
* Build a reputation within the community

***

### ⚠️ Best Practices

When using Toolbox assets, keep these tips in mind:

* **Check Permissions & Licensing** – Make sure you’re allowed to use the asset in your project
* **Review Quality** – Not all assets are optimized or secure
* **Avoid Over-Reliance** – Customize assets to make your experience unique
* **Test Before Shipping** – Ensure compatibility with your game
