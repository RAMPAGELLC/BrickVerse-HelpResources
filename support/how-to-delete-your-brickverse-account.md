# How to Delete Your BrickVerse Account

If you’d like to permanently delete your BrickVerse account, you’ll need to submit a request to our support team. This helps ensure your account is protected and only removed by its rightful owner.

#### 📩 Requesting Account Deletion

You can request deletion in one of the following ways:

**Option 1: Submit a Support Ticket**

* Go to [**help.brickverse.gg**](https://help.brickverse.gg)
* Log in to your account
* Open a new support ticket requesting account deletion

**Option 2: Send an Email**

* Email [**support@brickverse.gg**](mailto:support@brickverse.gg)
* Use the **same email address associated with your BrickVerse account**

#### 🔐 Verification Process

For security reasons, our staff will:

* Verify that you own the account
* May request additional information if needed

This step ensures no one can delete your account without proper authorization.

#### ⚠️ Important Notes

* Account deletion is **permanent and cannot be undone**
* All associated data may be removed as part of the process
* Processing time may vary depending on verification

***

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out through the support portal.
